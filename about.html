About Me

I am an experienced Technical Systems Professional with over 9 years of expertise in optimizing operations and managing complex technical infrastructures, now transitioning into data science. Through my career managing inventory systems, equipment performance analytics, and operational databases, I've developed strong foundations in data analysis, statistical reporting, and process optimization that naturally align with data science principles.

My technical background spans across energy, logistics, and technology sectors, where I've consistently leveraged data-driven approaches to achieve measurable improvements - including reducing service response times by 35% and decreasing inventory discrepancies by 15%. This operational experience, combined with my recent intensive training in Python programming, statistical analysis, and machine learning fundamentals through Dibimbing and Dicoding certification, positions me uniquely at the intersection of domain expertise and emerging analytical capabilities.

What truly excites me about transitioning into data science is the opportunity to apply advanced analytical techniques to solve complex business problems at scale. My hands-on experience with large operational datasets, performance metrics, and system optimization provides me with invaluable context that many traditional data science candidates may lack. I understand not just how to analyze data, but how that analysis translates into real-world operational impact.

I am actively expanding my technical toolkit through continuous learning in machine learning algorithms, data visualization with Python, and advanced statistical modeling. My goal is to bridge the gap between technical operations and strategic data insights, helping organizations unlock the full potential of their operational data to drive efficiency, reduce costs, and enhance decision-making processes.

If you're looking for a data professional who combines deep operational experience with fresh analytical perspectives, and who understands both the technical infrastructure that generates data and the business context that gives it meaning, I would welcome the opportunity to discuss how my unique background can contribute to your data science initiatives.
